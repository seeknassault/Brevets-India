THE SKYBUS PROJECT

The "Skybus" devised by Konkan Railway Corporation Ltd., has been seen as an innovative, eco-friendly and cost-effective solution to the transportation problems of metro cities. This is an outcome of the research and development by Prof. S C Lakkad, Department of Aerospace Engineering. It consists of an elevated rail system laid on a set of pillars and tracks. A regular bogey moves on the rail track and the coaches are suspended from the bogey. IIT Bombay has been involved in the design and development of composite Skybus coaches under a TIFAC Project. Fabricated by Kineco Ltd., Goa, the prototype of the coach is designed with:
❁ A stainless steel skeleton with FRP side, roof and side panelling for corrosion resistance coupled with light weight.
❁ A four metre wide door for quick discharge of passengers.
